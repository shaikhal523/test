History of The Skin Lab Shop


Welcome to SkinLab. For over 24 years we have been your number 1 destination for premium beauty, delivering the latest in innovative clinical skincare and luxury spa products. We were one of the first online stores to offer dermatologist-created and recommended products worldwide. We offer free, fast shipping worldwide. Here at SkinLab you have access to over 8000 products across more than 300 brands including hair, cosmetics, electrical, fragrance and of course skincare.



Our Vision


Our vision - is to be the number one choice for skincare, health and well-being to help people feel more confident about their appearance.



The Mission


Ultimately we will provide you with honest, open and transparent professional advice to deliver you a holistic approach towards your specific needs. Everyone involved with Skin Lab has a responsibility and duty to deliver on the following:

1) Educating patients and clients to help them make the right choices

2) To offer customers and clients suitable treatment/price plans

3) Structured approach to assessment of customers needs
